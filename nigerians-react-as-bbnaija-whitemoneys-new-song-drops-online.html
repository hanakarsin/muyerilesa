Nigerians React As BBNaija Whitemoney's New Song Drops Online

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Saturday, May 18, 2024

Winner of the Big Brother Naija Season 6 Edition Hazel Onyeze popularly known as Whitemoney has caused a stir online with his new single featuring an indigenous rapper, Zoro.

Whitemeoney released the new single titled “NÀ WE-WE” alongside the new songs of Davido, Zinolessky, Asake and Burna Boy.

The new song of the reality TV star is a follow-up to his previously released solo single ‘Selense’ which was later remixed with Tamy Moyo.

However, many Nigerians have expressed disappointment at Whitemoney with claims that the song is below what they had expected.

Many Nigerians have advised him to leave music and choose a new career between a celebrity chef or celebrity barber so he doesn’t waste his money on music productions.

@90sSophie wrote: “Davido, Burna boy, Asake and Zino no disappoint walai. As for whitemoney, who allow am record that song for studio?”

@_AsiwajuLerry wrote: “I think I speak for everyone when I say Zinoleesky released the best song out of all the expected drops. But as for Whitemoney, I leave you to God”

@SirDavidBent wrote: “Whitemoney should have just concentrated on becoming a celebrity barber or chef. That would have been a better career path than going into music. The last song he released was not so bad. But this one is way below the standard. All these BBNaija celebs and delusion”

@Kelvinismael72 wrote: “I have a friend who has little ear challenge he finds it difficult to hear very clearly, this morning he came to my house I mistakenly played Whitemoney song and he’s now deaf”.
