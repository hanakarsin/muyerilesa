Engagement invitation message ideas for your ring ceremony

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Sunday, July 7, 2024

Engagement parties are significant as they mark the union between two lovers. The presence of the duo's friends and family makes everything sentimental and memorable. Using warm and festive words is important when sending your engagement invitation message.

An engagement ceremony is marked by exchanging rings as a vow of committing to a serious relationship. This is followed by a feast for the duo and their guests. Since it is an important part of the lovers' journey, it is necessary to select their invitees thoughtfully. Therefore, the engagement invitation message should also express how glad their presence will make you feel.

Unique engagement invitation wording

Engagement party invitation messages should be kept short but sweet. Since it does not include nearly as much as your wedding invitation, don't feel pressured to write a lot. So, how do you invite someone for engagement? Here is a list of simple engagement invitation messages you can try out:

  • Our journey of becoming one is around the corner. Therefore, we humbly invite you to celebrate our ring ceremony with us.
  • Together (bride and groom’s name) are cordially inviting you to our engagement party. Your presence will be a great honour.
  • We cordially invite you to celebrate with us as we mark our journey to being one official. See you soon.
  • Your presence will be highly valued as we celebrate the engagement of our daughter (name of the bride to be) with (name of groom to be) on (venue) at (location).
  • We are delighted to announce our ring ceremony. We would be pleased to have you join us for the party on (date) at (location).
  • It started with a smile and a kiss, and now it's time to celebrate. So kindly join us as we celebrate our ring party.

Simple ring ceremony invitation text message

Love is a beautiful journey where two people decide to be with each other. However, when taking the union to another level, inviting people to witness the engagement event is a lovely addition to the story.

If you are having difficulty finding the best engagement party invitation wording, here are a few invitations for engagement ceremony samples to consider:

  • As our two hearts beat as one, eventually, we decided to exchange our wedding vows. We invite you to see God's doings at our residence at our ring party on (date).
  • Our daughter (bride's name) and son (groom's name) are having one of the most memorable moments in life. Please come and celebrate with them.
  • Your presence will make our ring party fun, enjoyable and memorable. So kindly bless us with your presence at (venue) on (date).
  • As one of the most important people in our life, we request your presence during our ring ceremony at (venue) on (date).
  • I couldn't imagine a lonely ring party without family and friends. So come and be part of this special day for us.
  • Finally, I am getting engaged to the man of my dreams. However, the party would be incomplete without you. So please join us for the celebration at (venue) on (date).
  • My ring party will be held at (venue) on (date). I expect to see you and your family as we celebrate this monumental day.
