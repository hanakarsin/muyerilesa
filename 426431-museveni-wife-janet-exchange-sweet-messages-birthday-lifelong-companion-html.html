Museveni and Wife Janet Exchange Sweet Messages as Ugandan President Marks Birthday: &quot;My Lifelong Co

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Friday, July 26, 2024
  • Yoweri Museveni celebrated turning a year older on Wednesday, September 15
  • As he turned 77 years old, his loving wife, Janet Kataaha, took to Twitter to pen him a lovely message
  • Museveni responded with another beautiful post, thanking his wife for being his pillar of life for the 48 years they have stayed together as a couple

Ugandan President Yoweri Kaguta Museveni and his wife, First Lady Janet Kataaha exchanged lovely messages on the President's birthday.

Museveni celebrated turning 77 years old on Wednesday, September 15, and his wife Janet could not be left out in celebrating her husband.

Going on Twitter, the First Lady publicly wished her husband a happy birthday, attaching a beautiful illuminated photo of them walking a path together and wrote:

"Dear @KagutaMuseveni, we are continually thankful for your life, health, strength and for every kindness of God towards you. May the blessings of the Lord always surround and pursue you as you lead our family and lead the country. Have a wonderful birthday and a blessed year."

A few minutes later, her darling husband, with another beautiful photo of them exiting a wooden-doored building adorned in celebratory decor, said in response:

"Thank you so much Maama! Even on this day, I continue to praise God for giving me the precious gift that you are. You are my cornerstone, my best friend, my lifelong companion! I look forward to reaching 100 together."

Museveni pleads with daughters

In other news, the Ugandan President hilariously narrated how his daughters have joined their mother in 'roasting' their hair.

Via Twitter, he said that if they continue to roast their hair they would finish him before he hit 100 years.

I am safe," he narrated in a hilarious tweet.
