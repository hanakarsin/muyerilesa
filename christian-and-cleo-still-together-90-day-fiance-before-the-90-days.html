Here's Where '90 Fianc' Stars Christian and Cleo's Relationship Stands

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Christian and Cleo from '90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days' are still together. During the tell-all, Christian revealed his true intentions for Cleo.

The Gist:

  • Christian and Cleo reportedly met on Instagram.
  • Their relationship has stumbled on rocky terrain on multiple occasions.
  • Christian shocked fans when he revealed his intentions for Cleo during the 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days: First Look Tell All.

Love knows no bounds — just ask the cast of TLC's reality TV franchise 90 Day Fiancé, who are willing to put it all on the line. In Before the 90 Days, viewers see couples travel abroad to connect with their long-distance lovers and hopefully live Happily Ever After. However, some couples haven’t been as lucky as others.

In the June 2023 premiere of Before the 90 Days, we were reunited with a few familiar faces, including fan favorites like Gino and Jasmine, and several newcomers — including, but not limited to, Christian Allgood and Cleo Corraine.

The latter hails from Minnesota while the former lives in England. But will their romance be enough to transcend the Atlantic? If you’re dying to know whether or not Christian and Cleo are still together post-production, you’re not the only one!

Christian and Cleo from ‘90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days’ are still together … but there's more to their story.

After the 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days tell-all episode aired in October 2023, it has become clear that Christian and Cleo are still together. However, that doesn't mean it's all sunshine in paradise. Christian's flirtatious and chatty personality has certainly landed him in hot water more than once and has even made it difficult for Cleo to trust him.

In fact, many 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days cast members seem to think Christian is a narcissist who loves attention and is really into himself. Despite how others might interpret it, Christian doesn't seem to think he's flirtatious but instead, just someone who wants to interact with people and make friends.

While there's certainly nothing wrong with wanting to meet people and remain social (it's become rather challenging these days), Christian's bar encounter with Debra somewhat proves his intentions when out and about may be misleading. After Debra was brought on to speak during the tell-all, she admitted that if she didn't know he was involved with someone, she would think it was "100 percent flirtation."

Although Christian may overextend himself when it comes to being friendly with others, he revealed his intentions for Cleo, and that's to eventually marry her. Cleo traveled to the U.S. to meet some Christian's family and he wants her to move to the U.S. While Christian didn't give a full-on proposal during the tell-all, he did ask Cleo if she would want to marry him and said that she can expect it to happen soon.

Of course, Christian's hesitation to actually propose on TV was met with some criticism, but he still gets some credit for wanting to pop the question in-person.

A look back at Christian and Cleo’s ‘90 Day Fiancé’ love story.

Christian is a party animal who’s ready to settle down with someone special, and that someone just might be Cleo. That said, it won’t be easy. This is especially true given that Cleo is transgender, making her the second trans person to ever join the franchise.

The revelation comes as a shock to Christian’s down-home, traditional family, who are hesitant to accept his relationship with Cleo for that reason.

But, now that Christian has made it clear that he is considering taking things to the next level with Cleo, only time will tell if these two love birds have what it takes to hold onto their relationship.
