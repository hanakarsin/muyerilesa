Barely 48 Hours After BBNaija Win, Whitemoney Achieves Another Milestone

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Big Brother Naija ‘shine ye eye’ winner, Hayel Oyeze Onou better known as White money has hit one million followers on his Instagram page barely 48 hours after the reality show ended.

The drastic increase in followers came after the Enugu-born ex-housemate was declared winner of the shine ye eye edition of the show, on Sunday, 3rd October.

White money no doubt won the heart of viewers throughout his stay in the house which has been reflected in the rapid increase in followers on Instagram.

However, the most followed housemate in the Shine Ye Eye edition is Liquorose who has a massive following of over 1.4 million as at the time of filing this report.

Naija News earlier reported that the organizers of Big Brother Naija on Monday presented 30 million Naira Whitemoney.

The reality tv star was also presented with a house key and a car by the organizers of the highly watched show.

Among the prizes, Whitemoney got on Monday was a ₦30m cash prize, cash in an Abeg digital wallet and bitcoins courtesy of Patricia, a two-bedroom apartment courtesy of RevolutionPlus Property, a top of range SUV from Innoson Motors, and a sponsored trip for two.
