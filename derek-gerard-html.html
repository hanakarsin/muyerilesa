Derek Gerard - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, June 6, 2024

Creator of comedic video content for YouTube who published the popular videos "I'm Going To Jail. (Full Explanation)" and "How to Live Stream on Twitch FOR FREE! 2015!" Some of his most popular videos have been food challenge videos. 

Before Fame

He first signed up for YouTube with the name VideoGameAssKickers in September of 2010. He uploaded a first video, called "Fifa 14 AFTER DENTIST..," in November of 2013.

Trivia

He has accumulated more than 2 million subscribers to his YouTube channel. His derek_gerard Instagram account has featured multiple photographs of his dog. 

Family Life

He is a Canadian YouTuber. He has a sister named Neela Maria.

Associated With

He has posted reactions to music videos by Ariana Grande and Nicki Minaj.
