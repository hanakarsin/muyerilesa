Senator Goje shows off his new wife, Aminatu Dahiru Binani

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Saturday, March 2, 2024

- Senator Danjuma Goje is the latest husband in town and he is loving it

- The love-struck senator has shared a photo of his beautiful wife on his social media pages

- His new bride, Aminatu Dahiru Binani, is a self-made billionaire who oversees a thriving conglomerate in northern Nigeria

Senator Danjuma Goje has, for the first time, shared photos of his new wife, Aminatu Dahiru Binani on social media.

Goje, a former governor of Gombe and political leader of the ruling All Progressives Congress (APC) in the state, got married to Aminatu on Friday, October 2.

Interestingly, Aminatu is a sister to another senator representing Adama Central Senatorial District in the Nigerian Senate, Engineer Aisha Dahiru Binani.

She is also the current Group Managing Director of the Binani Group of Companies, a thriving conglomerate in northern Nigeria.

The young lady has been described as a self-made billionaire by a dint of her hard work in the private sector over the years.

In the beautiful photos shared on his social media pages, the senator and his new bride were all smiles as they posed for the camera.

Apart from his giant strides as a politician which connects him to the grassroots, the senator largely lives a quiet and peaceful life.

Due to his popularity and the respect he commands from his political associates and friends, Goje was honoured with the presence of prominent Nigerians at his wedding.

Among those who attended the ceremony were Senate President, Ahmed Lawan; Governors Muhammad Inuwa Yahaya of Gombe and Abdullahi Ganduje of Kano.

The Secretary to the Government of the Federation (SGF), Boss Mustapha was also present as well as the minister of communications and digital economy, Dr Isa Pantami and his police affairs counterpart, Mohammed Maigari Dingyadi.

Senators Ahmed Yerima, Aliyu Magatakarda Wamakko, Gabriel Suswam, Abdullahi Adamu and a former governor of Jigawa state, Sule Lamido were also present.

Also present was the former SGF, Alhaji Yahaya Ahmed and the new executive director of Federal Housing Authority, Abdulmumin Jibrin.

Recall that two years ago, Hajiya Yelwa Danjuma Goje, wife of the senator, died in a hospital in the United States of America.

Ahmed Mohammed Goje, son of the former governor announced the death of his mother on Monday, October 30, 2018.

Legit.ng had reported that dignitaries from across the country visited the revered senator in his Abuja home to commiserate with him.

Among those who visited Senator Goje then were the governors of Sokoto, Katsina, Kaduna and Zamfara.
