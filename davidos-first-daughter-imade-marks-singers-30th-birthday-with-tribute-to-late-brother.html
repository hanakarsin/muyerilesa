Davido's First Daughter, Imade Marks Singer's 30th Birthday With Tribute To Late Brother

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Davido’s first daughter, Imade has taken to social media to celebrate her father on his 30th birthday today, November 21st, 2022, and also pay a tribute to her late brother, Ifeanyi Adeleke.

Naija News reports that the award-winning singer, David Adeleke, popularly known as Davido turned 30 today.

To celebrate her father, 7-year-old Imade via her Instagram sent kind wishes to him with a family photo of herself, the singer, and his other two kids, including the late Ifeanyi Adeleke.

Sharing the photo, seven years old Imade Adeleke wrote: “Happy birthday daddy! I wish you peace, joy & love ❤️.”

Davido’s birthday is coming less than a month after the singer lost his first son, Ifeanyi Adeleke, tragically, in a drowning accident at his Ikoyi mansion. 

Following Ifeanyi’s death, the singer had stayed away from social media ever since.

Despite his social media absence, fans and celebrities alike have taken to different social mediums to celebrate him on his special day today.
