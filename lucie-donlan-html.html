Lucie Rose Donlan - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Friday, March 15, 2024

Reality television personality who is predominantly recognized for having been cast on the 2019 season of ITV's Love Island. She is also wildly popular on social media, boasting a staggering 1.7 million Instagram followers.

Before Fame

She was a professional surfer and model.

Trivia

She has been featured in magazines such as High Tide and Wed.

Family Life

She was once in a relationship with Charlie Frederick. She and Luke Mabbott have been dating since July 2020. They announced their engagement in December 2021.

Associated With

She competed on Love Island which was hosted by Caroline Flack.
