How Pastor Impregnated Woman &amp; Daughter

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Friday, May 17, 2024

The pastor of a popular Pentecostal Church located along Nnebisi Road, Asaba, Delta State has allegedly impregnated a 45-year-old woman and her 14-year-old daughter.

The pastor identified as Pastor Sam who is currently on the run was said to have impregnated them under the guise of conducting prayers for them to deliver them of evil spirits that had been battling them.

According to report, after the death of her husband, the mother of three and her daughter have been under evil spells after elders of the Illah community where they live, had consulted seers who accused the woman and her teenage daughter of having a hand in his death.

The woman and her daughter were introduced to Pastor Sam in December 2013 and he had promised to deliver them from the spell. Thye have been consulting with the pastor until last week when they both took ill and were taken to a private hospital in Asaba by their neighbours.

After several tests at the hospital, the doctors confirmed both of them pregnant and they claimed the pastor was responsible.

After the news of the pregnancy broke out, the pastor was said to have deserted his church and left an unknown destination.

Elizabeth Agu, the elder sister of the pregnant woman who confirmed the incident said, they've been trying to reach the pastor on his mobile phone but its been futile as his phone switched off and his whereabouts unknown.
