How much is GOtv per month in Kenya?

If you are planning to buy a GOtv, set-top box, then be assured you will get lots of entertainment with local channels made in Africa and for Africa.

Since you have your preference, GOtv has also categorised its services in four different bouquets or packages, all with varying subscription prices. So what are the different GOtv paybill packages you can settle on?

GOtv Lite

According to the official website, the GOtv Lite package is lite on price and big on quality. And one can go for the package's annual, quarterly, or monthly subscription. In addition, clients get 21 channels that include one for music, one for sports, and 19 for other genres.

The annual package costs Ksh.1,525; quarterly will cost you Ksh.610, while the monthly one will cost you Ksh.255.

GOtv value package

In this bouquet, you get 30+ channels for Ksh.510 per month. This is ideal for individuals looking to enjoy sports as the bouquet gives clients two sports channels.

In this bouquet, you get 40+ channels. These include two music channels, three sports channels, two movie channels, and 40 other genres. The package will cost you Ksh.815 monthly.

GOtv Max

GOtv Max is the most expensive bouquet and costs Ksh.1,070 monthly. Clients get 50+ channels when they pay for the subscription. In addition, they can access four music channels, six sports channels, three movie channels, and 47 other genres.

How do I pay for GOtv Kenya?

There are several methods of how to pay GOtv in Kenya. You can either pay via M-Pesa, Pesapal, or credit card. However, mobile money is the widely used method because of its convenience and simplicity, and at the same time, saving you that precious time.

How to pay GOtv via M-Pesa

To make your payment via M-Pesa, all you require is the GOtv paybill number in Kenya and your GOtv IUC number. You find the IUC number at the bottom of your GOtv set-top box, printed on a red sticker. Below is the procedure to complete your M-Pesa payment transaction.

  • Open your M-Pesa menu, and tap on Lipa Na M-Pesa.
  • Select Pay Bill, and enter the GOtv paybill no 423655 in the business number field, and press OK.
  • In the account number field, enter your GOtv Basket ID (reference number) and press OK.
  • Now enter the amount to pay according to the bouquet you are subscribing for, and press OK.
  • Key in your M-Pesa PIN and, again, press OK to send and complete your transaction.
  • Confirm that your transaction is going to Multichoice through the hakikisha prompt message.
  • You will get a verification message to confirm your money is received.

Paying for your GOtv using Pesapal

Pesapal is another excellent avenue for paying for your GOtv subscription. All you have to do is to download the app or sign in online and click on pay. You can use the following steps on the mobile application.

  • Open the app and click on TV.
  • Choose GOtv from the available options.
  • Enter your account number and amount you want to pay and press continue.
  • Choose the preferred payment method (either M-Pesa or credit card) on the next screen and click continue.
  • If you chose M-Pesa, you would get an STK push payment pop up requesting you to write your PIN. Enter your PIN to finalise the payment.
  • If you choose the credit card method, the app will deduct the amount from your bank account balance.
  • You will get a message once the transaction is complete.

Credit card payments

You don’t have to cram the GOtv paybill number Kenya as clients can now submit their subscriptions through their credit card. Craft Silicon powers the service. All you have to do is to sign in to your account and click on pay. You will get a list of online payment methods. Choose credit card payment powered by Craft Silicon and follow the instructions.

Bank payments

GOtv has partnered with various banks to allows customers to submit their payments easily. And the process is different with every bank's payment platform, either their official mobile app or USSD code.

How to check GOtv subscription and clear error codes

Checking your GOtv subscription and account status is quite simple. With almost every household having a smartphone, you can check your subscription online and on your phone.

Using your phone's browser, visit the GOtv self-service page, sign in using your surname or phone number, followed by your IUC number. After successfully logging in, you can check your account status and the due date for the next payment.
