Ella Thomas (Reality Star) - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Reality star and award-winning model from Glasgow who's most recognized as being a contestant on season 10 of Love Island UK. She was coupled with Tyrique Hyde from day one. She has done photoshoots with the Colours Agency. 

Before Fame

When she was 12 years old, she was an extra in the film World War Z. This is how she met Brad Pitt.

Trivia

She was named Female Model of the Year at the Scottish media awards in October of 2022. She has starred in music videos for artists such as Headie One and Burna Boy. Her ellathomas_ Instagram account has garnered 950,000 followers. She has done modeling work for brands such as PrettyLittleThing.

Family Life

She was born in Glasgow, Scotland in the UK, but currently lives in London. She has been dating Tyrique Hyde.

Associated With

She and Ruchee Gurung both appeared on season 10 of Love Island.
