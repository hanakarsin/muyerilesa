Why I Did Not Meet Obaseki During My Recent Visit To Edo PDP Delegates

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Sunday, July 21, 2024

Governor Nyesom Wike of Rivers State has given reasons why he did not meet with Edo State Governor Godwin Obaseki during his recent visit to the state.

Naija News reports that Wike earlier in the week met with some PDP delegates at the residence of a former Chairman of the Board of Trustees (BoT), the late Chief Tony Anenih.

The Rivers state governor had been in the state for presidential campaign consultations, but did not meet with Obaseki or his deputy, Philip Shaibu.

In an interview with BBC News Pidgin, Wike explained his reasons.

“I did not visit Governor Godwin Obaseki because the People’s Democratic Party (PDP) did not send me there,” he explained.

“They despatched me to meet with delegates (rather than the Governor) to discuss how our party will win the presidential race in 2023.

“It’s nowhere written that I have to go via the Governor before seeing the party delegates.

“Governor Godwin Obaseki is not in a relationship with me, he is not my girlfriend, so why should I be quarreling with him?”

Recall that Wike and Obaseki had recently engage in a public clash, after he Rivers governor attacked Shaibu.
