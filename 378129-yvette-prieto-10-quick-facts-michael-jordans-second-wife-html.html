5. Her family got into trouble with the law

Prieto has been one of those celebrities who are nearly squeaky clean, with no skeletons in the closet. However, during her early years, she was at the centre of a crime involving her family. In 2001, her parents and other people working in their family company were accused of insurance fraud.

Prieto was not incriminated in the insurance fraud. At the time, she had already left the company. And considering that she declared bankruptcy a while later, it is safe to assume that she did not benefit from it in any way.
