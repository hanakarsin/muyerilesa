BBNaija All Stars: What If I Get Evicted A Day Before The Show Ends

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Friday, August 16, 2024

Influencer and socialite, Tacha has again disclosed the reason she refused to feature in the Big Brother Naija All Stars season.

Naija News recalls that Tacha had earlier stated that she could not make an appearance on the show because the organisers were not ready to pay.

Speaking once again on her decision to shun the reality show during an interview on Cool FM, Tacha stated that she ought to get paid to feature on the reality show because she can’t water down her brand to give Nigerians a show.

She also claimed that a personality like hers is rare and Nigerians don’t usually get people as entertaining as her.

Speaking further, Tacha opined that if she gets evicted or disqualified again on the show, she doesn’t want to leave empty-handed.

I Will Take Mercy Down If She Tries Anything Funny – Ike

Meanwhile, Big Brother Naija All Stars housemate, Ike Onyema, has threatened to retaliate if his ex-lover and fellow housemate, Mercy Eke tries to work against him.

Ike stated this while speaking with his fellow housemate, Angel Smith on the ongoing BBNaija All Stars reality show.

He, however, threatened that he would not hesitate to retaliate if she works against him.

According to him, he would form an alliance and take her down.

Ike said that he feels like Mercy would put him up for eviction.

He said “I have a feeling that Mercy wanted to nominate me and I don’t know why.

“But If she tries anything funny I will form alliances and we will take her down, I will make sure she doesn’t win, sheybi her fans get money ni, they will vote tire.”
