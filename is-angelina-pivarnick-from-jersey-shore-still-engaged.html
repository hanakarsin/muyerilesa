Is Angelina From Jersey Shore Still Engaged? Lets Take a Look at Her Dating Life

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

Is Angelina Pivarnick from 'Jersey Shore' still engaged? Here's what we know about her romantic status, including whether she's still betrothed.

Reality star Angelina Pivarnick is most known for starring in Jersey Shore: Family Vacation. As fans have gotten to know her since the original Jersey Shore series back in 2009, viewers are wondering about her dating life.

Is Angelina engaged? And if so, who is she engaged to? Has she been married before? Here are all of the details about the popular reality TV star and her love life.

Is Angelina from 'Jersey Shore' still engaged?

Angelina is engaged to her fiancé Vinny Tortorella, who she has publicly been in a relationship with since early 2023. He popped the question on an episode of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation.

In front of the entire cast of the show, Vinny asked Angelina to marry him, and she said "yes." During the proposal he said, "A lot of things in life aren't guaranteed, but something that I've learned since I met you is loving you is gonna be guaranteed for the rest of my life."

He also said, "I want to grow old with you, I want to experience everything with you. I wouldn't want to experience it with anyone else — together, as one."

Vinny doesn't have any public social media accounts, and aside from the show, he seems to stay out of the public eye.

In August 2023, a police report showed that Angelina called emergency services on Vinny, according to Page Six. The allegations included domestic violence, but she didn't press any charges against him at the time.

“I can confirm that Angelina did in fact call the police to her home. After speaking with the officers, Angelina decided not to file charges,” Pivarnick’s attorney told Page Six. “This matter is now closed. Moving forward, Angelina would ask for privacy as it pertains to her personal life.”

According to Vinny's attorney, they are still engaged and living together. In fact, he denies the accusation of domestic violence.

Although Angelina doesn't appear to have a wedding date set yet, she did reveal to E! News in May 2023 that she isn't going big the second time around (yes, this will be her second marriage). The Jersey Shore alum also admitted, "I don't think I would have the girls. I just wouldn't," referring to co-stars Jenni "JWoww" Farley, Nicole "Snooki" Polizzi and Deena Cortese. Angelina added, "I wouldn't incorporate them as bridesmaids this time."

Angelina Pivarnick has been married before.

This isn't the first time Angelina has been engaged. In fact, she was married to Christopher Larangeira. The couple got married in 2019, but ultimately split in 2022.

Christopher isn't really in the limelight, but he does have a public Instagram account under the name @chris_e_piss_e. Angelina has opened up about their divorce publicly.

She said, "I never want to say anything bad about my ex, but we just had different paths in life. That’s just what happens sometimes. I’m happy that I’m on the other side of it. I’m seeing much more clear now.”

Before marrying Christopher, Angelina dated Food Network star Chris Nirschel. The couple made an appearance together on Season 1 of the VH1 series Couples Therapy. The two ultimately weren't able to pursue their relationship any further and called it quits.

Some call Angelina Pivarnick's fiancé "Vinny 2.0." Here's why.

Over the years, fans have suspected that Angelina was in a secret relationship with her Jersey Shore co-star Vinny Guadagnino. So much so that viewers have nicknamed her current fiancé "Vinny 2.0."

Although both of the cast members have denied these rumors, Vinny did add fuel to the fire back in 2021 — when Angelina was still with her ex-husband Chris — with a social media post. He took to Twitter and said, "Don’t worry Angelina if it doesn’t work out, you always have me."

Season 7 of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation first started airing on Aug. 3, 2023. Fans can watch Thursdays at 8 p.m. EST on MTV. They can also stream on Hulu or Paramount+.
