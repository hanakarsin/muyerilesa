Ex-Machachari Actor MaDVD Shares Throwback Photos of Acting Days, Winning Chaguo La Teeniez Awards

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, June 14, 2024
  • Kenyan actor Brian Kimaru alias MaDVD posted throwback photos while on the Machachari set
  • The photos shared by the actor, who is now based in the UK, featured his ex-castmates Baha, Govi and Stella
  • Another image depicted the celebrated actor posing with two Chaguo La Teeniez Awards

Actor Brian Kimaru, who famously featured on the now-defunct Citizen TV show Machachari has reminisced on the good old days.

Via his Instagram stories, the actor shared numerous throwback photos on Monday, August 23, 2021.

In one of the photos shared by MaDVD, currently based in the UK, he was beaming at the camera alongside Tyler Mbaya alias Baha, Malik Lemuel aka Govi and Natasha Ngegie, who played Stella.

He hinted they are still good pals as he attached Jay Z and Kanye west's hit Never Let Me Down to the image.
