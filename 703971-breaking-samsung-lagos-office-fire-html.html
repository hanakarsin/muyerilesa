BREAKING: Samsung Digital Centre On Fire (PHOTOS)

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Thursday, April 18, 2024

Samsung Digital Centre on Allen road, Ikeja, Lagos state is on fire, Legit.ng can authoritatively report.

[article_adwert]

The cause of the fire which started around 12.15 pm is not known but according to eye witnesses, the fire started from the company's service centre.

As at the time of filing this report, the building was still in flames as customers and staff of the company scampered away for their lives packing goods from the building.

The Lagos state fire service men arrived the scene at 1.07 pm.

However, no casualties have been reported, as the fire outbreak has led to heavy gridlock in the area.
