NCDC Records 1270 New Cases Of COVID-19

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, August 11, 2024

The Nigeria Centre for Disease Control (NCDC) has reported 1270 new cases of the novel coronavirus (COVID-19) in Nigeria.

Naija News reports that the new cases were reported in a post on the disease control agency’s Twitter handle on Tuesday night, January 12, 2021.

The newly reported cases bring the total number of confirmed COVID-19 cases in Nigeria to 102,601 which includes 81,574 discharged cases and 1,373 deaths.

Below is the breakdown of the latest cases according to states with Lagos State having the highest number of cases followed by Abuja, the Federal Capital Territory (FCT).
