Man stunned after robbers rummage through stolen handbag in full glare of passengers

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Sunday, June 30, 2024

A South African man was recently thrown aback after thugs stormed the taxi he was traveling in straight from a crime scene and started ransacking a bags they had stolen.

According to a Twitter user named Letsoalo, the thieves got into the taxi in Pretoria and could not contain their excitement as they started rummaging through the stolen goods in front of everyone aboard.

Letsoalo captured everything on camera and shared the same on Twitter with the caption;

"These two guys stole a bag and got in the same taxi we were in. They started searching the bag and found a laptop, a purse with some money and cards (probably license too), some papers. At Pretoria CBD".

The video angered many social media users especially South Africans who slammed the driver for driving the thugs to their destination.

Others, however, picked issue with Letsoalo whom they accused of staying mum instead of ringing the police.
