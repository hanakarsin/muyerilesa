Mamoudou Athie - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Tuesday, April 16, 2024

Mauritanian-American actor notable for his roles in The Circle, Unicorn Store, The Front Runner, Underwater and Jurassic World 3. On television, he played Matt Greer in Sorry for Your Loss, Jerome in Cake, Carl in The Detour, Grandmaster Flash in The Get Down and the title character in Oh Jerome, No.

Before Fame

He studied his craft at the William Esper Studio and at the Yale University School of Drama.

Trivia

He performed on a song called "Wake Up, Sheep!" for the 2017 independent film Patti Cake$.

Family Life

He is the son of a diplomat father who received political asylum in the United States.

Associated With

He starred alongside Kristen Stewart in the movie Underwater.
