H_Art the Band Grateful to Jalang'o for Supporting Them in Production of Their My Jaber Video: &quot;God

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Thursday, July 4, 2024
  • H_art the Band launched their third album, which they have been popularising for the past two months
  • The album comprises of six tracks, and one of the tracks is "My Jaber", which many feel is the emblem of the whole album
  • The group thanked Jalang'o for supporting them in the production of "My Jaber" official video, particularly paying for the fashion styling therein

Three-man band, H_art the band, has expressed its gratitude to radio host and comic Jalang'o after he offered to be part of the success of the group's latest afro-benga hit, My Jaber.

The group is currently popularising its third studio album, Simple Man, in which the "My Jaber" hit is borne.

According to them, Jalang'o fell in favour with the track and offered to be behind the fashion styling in the song's official music video.

"Let’s be honest, ujaluo ni gharama. Our brother Jalang'o loved ‘My Jaber’ so much he paid for all the styling in this video. Brother, thank you a lot & God bless you. Best believe, when the industry comes together as this great things happen," said the group.

Other media personalities hailed by the group for support are Betty Kyalo and Citizen TV's Willis Raburu.

Just four days into premiering on YouTube, My Jaber has amassed more than one million views.

The band also owed their success to their fans, who have been part of their journey when their career began in 2013.

"We are grateful to our fans who have supported us since day one. When we first released 'Uliza Kiatu", they have subscribed, shared our music, attended our shows...we are grateful," Mordecai had said in an interview.

Simple Man album

As aforementioned, H_art the Band launched their third album, labelled Simple Man, since beginning their music career more than eight years ago.

The album comprises six jams that the group vouches for as greatest of all time and has other artistes incorporated.

Therein is Simple Man, My Jaber ft Kisumu's Brizy Annechild, Na Bado in which Nyashinski has been featured, Milele ft an SDA choir from Kisumu, Everyday and wait For You.

The album has been uploaded on the band's official website from where fans and music lovers can download it.

The group rose in 2014 when they released their first song, "Uliza Kiatu", which performed well and has continued to win hearts.

By their stage names, the band members are Mordecai Dex, Kenchez Muiya and Skoko Wachira Abednego.
