Jslutty - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Social media star who has risen to fame for his jsluttyy Instagram account. He has garnered massive popularity for his original sketch comedy clips often based around observational humor. His "J Slutty - "Vibe" (Official Video)" video has received over 4.5 million views since August of 2019. 

Before Fame

His real name is Jason. He began his Instagram account in February 2018.

Trivia

He has amassed more than 1.2 million followers to his Instagram account. He has also gained more than 550,000 subscribers to his Jsluttytofunny 123 Youtube channel. In November of 2021, he announced that he was doing his own NFT. 

Family Life

He has often featured his mother on his social media channels. He's been based in Chicago, Illinois. He posted a photo with his younger sister to his Instagram account in October 2020. His girlfriend Liyah Desha has also appeared on his social media. In January 2022, the couple welcomed a daughter.

Associated With

Funny Mike appeared in his YouTube video titled, "SURPRISING MIKE WITH THE ULTIMATE GIFTS FOR HIS BIRTHDAY."
