Relationship expert Benjamin Zulu lists 3 types of women a man should never date

- The relationship coach named the charismatic or alluring woman, who one would love only for her looks and great chemistry, as a no go zone

- The love doctor also said a codependent women should be avoided as they are great but have nothing to offer

During an interview with Switch TV's Mary Mwikali on Full Circle, Zulu said his opinion was based on his own experiences and urged young men to not only take chemistry into consideration when finding true love.

The first type of woman Zulu said should be avoided is the charismatic or alluring woman who one would love only for her looks and great chemistry.

"She's extremely sexually charged, irresistible. She's fine as a glass of wine, she commands attention. That's not a bad thing but the problem is that's all she is," he said.

Zulu said this type of woman hates what is regarded as female attributes in the house but adjusts very nicely to his man's biases.

The second type is the codependent woman who adores her man and gives him everything he wants but has nothing to offer.

The third type Zulu mentioned is the narcissistic woman who lacks empathy and is manipulative but will always know to act right when the two of you are alone.

He said she rushes intimacy, isolates the man from his friends, never says sorry and does not take criticism lightly hence making the man always feel guilty.

He said this type of woman will always hurry things to ensure the man is entangled.

She can hardly ask for money even though she is in need and does not cling to her man when he leaves her.
