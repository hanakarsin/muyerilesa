Adeboye, Ooni Of Ife Admonish Nigerians To Keep Praying And Praising God In Every Situation

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Saturday, May 25, 2024

The General Overseer of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), Pastor Enoch Adeboye, and the Ooni of Ife Oba Enitan Ogunwusi has admonished Nigerians to keep praying for the country as well as praising God in every situation they found themselves.

Naija News reports that Adeboye, who spoke in Ilorin, the Kwara state capital today during a sermon at the 12 hours marathon praise worship and thanksgiving, ‘YADAH 2022’ organised by the House of Prayer Assembly appealed to Nigerians not to desist from praising God in all situations in.

According to him, even though the condition in the country is harsh and unsuitable, they could be changed if the people engaged in praise worship and thanksgiving.

The RCCG GO, who quoted Job 2:1, from the Holy book during his sermon noted that even though Job in the bible didn’t commit any sin other than righteousness, God allowed the devil to try him and in the end, he won and his possessions were returned in double folds.

Adeboye’s sermon was delivered by the Regional Overseer of the RCCG, Pastor Nathaniel Adejumo, who told the audience that “the journey of life may not be smooth, but we should continue to praise God.”

Speaking on the theme, “Let all that I am praise the Lord,” Adeboye said people would continue to witness different situations in life, but they shouldn’t stop praise-worship and thanksgiving to God constantly, because He would change the situation for the better.

He said, “People should know the importance of praising God no matter the circumstances we find ourselves. Whether we are rich, happy or unhappy, ill or healthy, comfortable or uncomfortable; whether the economy is buoyant or lean, we should praise the Lord. The battle may be strong, but God never fails and he has never lost any battle. He triumphs after all tribulations.

“Continue to praise the Lord and let the devil go mad, and at the end, you will shame your enemies. Everything that is lost will be recovered; everything will turn around for good because God has never been a failure. God will change the situation. We should make sacrifices of praise to the Lord.”

Also, the Ooni of Ife, Oba Enitan Ogunwusi, at the gathering urged Nigerians to continue in their fervent prayers to God to make the country better.

The first-class monarch appreciated the Christian congregation for their prayers, which he said was sustaining the country.

Enitan, who advised people to live a life capable of making others happy said “I thank God for the life of Pastor Omorinoye, the founder of House of Prayer Assembly, and his wife, for living a life of making people happy.”
