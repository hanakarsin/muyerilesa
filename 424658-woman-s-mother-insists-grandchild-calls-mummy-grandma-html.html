Woman Says Ex's Mother Insists Grandchild Calls Her Mami Instead of Grandma

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, February 11, 2024
  • A US woman who has been in an on and off relationship with her baby daddy has said her former mother in law is overstepping
  • The mother said that she is confused as to why the older woman would want her granddaughter to call her mami
  • The lady sought advice online on how to handle the situation and politely ask the ex-mother in law to step back

A troubled woman has taken to the internet to seek advice on how to handle her ex-boyfriend's mother.

According to a report by Mirror, the pregnant woman said that her baby daddy's mother had previously questioned whether the child belongs to her son.

"Since then has said multiple times she will 'take her and raise her' if I don’t do it correctly. She also said she wanted our child to call her 'mami' I said politely no that I’m not comfortable with that it is essentially the same thing as Mommy/Mom," she was quoted by the Mirror saying.

In a related story, TUKO.co.ke reported how a Nairobi mum narrated enduring the pain of her mother-in-law trying to sabotage her marriage.

"My mother in law and sister in law hates me with passion.My husband is a graduate,well educated and has a well paying job while on the other hand after completing high school I didn't join college due to finances my family was struggling.I met my husband in a coffee shop I was working and he loved me right there and then," she penned.
