'Waka Waka' Singer Shakira Attacked by Pair of Wild Pigs in Barcelona

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Wednesday, February 21, 2024
  • Waka Waka singer Shakira has narrated how she was attacked by two wild pigs while walking with her son in Barcelona
  • The pop artist shares her eight-year-old son with her footballer bae Gerard Piqué who plays for soccer giants, Barcelona
  • The famous musician took to Instagram stories to let her fans in on the awkward moment that took place at a park in Spain

Pop singer Shakira has reportedly been attacked by two wild pigs. When the incident happened, the Waka Waka hitmaker walked with her son, 8, in Barcelona, Spain.

The Colombian-born singer's baby daddy is Gerard Piqué. He is a defender for the soccer club Barcelona.

She was taking a stroll with their son in the park when the pair of boars appeared from nowhere and grabbed her bag.

The BBC reports that the wild animals disappeared into the woods with the superstar's bag, which had her cellphone.

Then, according to the publication, she took to her Instagram stories to share her strange story with the world.

Her bag had been torn apart when she finally recovered it. The songstress, who performed in South Africa in 2010 during the FIFA Soccer World Cup, said the pigs destroyed "everything".

Shakira, who featured South African music band Freshlyground, sang Waka Waka in front of thousands of people during the opening and closing ceremonies of the soccer tournament.

The US thespian praised Khosi for slaying her role in Netflix's Blood & Water.

The 21-year-old portrays the character of Fikile Bhele in the drama series.

Gabrielle chose Khosi as her #WCW on Wednesday, 29 September.

The superstar shared that Khosi is on top of the game regardless of her age.

Taking to Twitter, the Deliver Us From Eva star urged her followers to stream Season 1 and 2 of Blood & Water so they could see Khosi in action. She captioned her post:

"Today’s #WCW is the immensely talented @Khosi_Ngema_. She plays Fikile Bhele on Netflix’s series Blood & Water (if you haven’t watched season 1, RUN DON’T WALK! Season 2 just started, and we are already deep). Khosi is only 21 years old and is already killing the game."

Tweeps, mainly from South Africa, took to Gabrielle's comment section to praise her for recognising the country's talent.
