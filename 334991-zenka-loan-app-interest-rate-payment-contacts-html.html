Zenka loan app, interest rate, payment, contacts

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Monday, January 1, 0001

There are so many mobile loan app lenders that have emerged in recent years to help Kenyans have access to quick cash to take care of their financing needs. Zenka loan app is one of the most popular mobile lenders in Kenya. It offers the fastest loan app solutions without the need for collateral.

Zenka loan app is the latest addition to the mobile loan applications in the country. Established by Zenka Kenya, the app has taken Kenyans by storm through the unique features that it offers to its customers. Unlike the other loan apps in the country, Zenka app allows customers to conveniently access a loan even if they do not own a smartphone.

Zenka loan app download and registration

Before you receive money in your Mpesa account, you are required to complete a simple registration and application process that begins with the Zenka app download from Google Playstore. After a successful download, follow this procedure to register with Zenka Kenya.

  • Open the app and key in your personal details including; first name, last name, National ID number, education level, source of income, net monthly income, email.
  • Select a security question and provide an answer to the security question
  • Create a 4-digit pin that you will use to log into the Zenka app
  • Click on the link shown to read the terms and conditions for the loan
  • After reading the terms and conditions, click on the button that reads “Agree and sign up”
  • You will receive a text message on your phone with a 4-digit verification code
  • Enter the code then click on done.

If you do not have a smartphone, then you do not need to panic because you can register for the Zenka loan application via USSD. All that you need to do is to dial *841# on your mobile phone and follow the prompts on your screen to register.

How to request for a loan on the Zenka loan app

After successful registration, you can proceed to apply for a loan using the following procedure;

  • Open the app
  • Click on the “Apply for a loan” menu button
  • Choose the loan amount that you wish to apply for and the loan repayment terms
  • Wait for the application to be evaluated by the Zenka team
  • If the loan application is successful, the loan amount will be sent directly to your mobile money account.

Alternatively, you can dial *841# on your phone and select the “Apply for a loan” option. You can follow the prompts to apply for the desired loan limit and the preferred repayment period.

How to repay the loan

You can only build your loan limit on Zenka if you pay the loan on time. Late repayment of the loan attracts a penalty of 1%. However, you can apply for a loan extension to postpone the due date of the loan to avoid such penalties. To repay the loan amount, follow these steps;

  • Open the M-Pesa menu on your phone
  • Choose the Lipa na Mpesa option
  • Choose Paybill
  • In the section that reads “Enter business number” enter the Zenka Paybill number, which is 979988
  • In the account number section, enter the phone number that you used to register with Zenka
  • Enter the loan amount that you want to repay
  • Enter your Mpesa pin to authorize the transaction
  • Wait for a confirmation SMS from Mpesa

Alternatively, you can repay the loan if you dial the USSD code *841# on your phone. You will be prompted to enter the 4-digit pin. Select the “Repay” menu option and follow the prompts to repay the loan.

Zenka loan limit

So, what is the minimum and maximum amount that you can receive if you borrow a loan using the Zenka loan app? If you have installed the Zenka loan app APK on your smartphone, you can enjoy a loan amount of between Kshs 500 and Kshs 20000.

Zenka loan interest rate

For the first time users, there is no interest fee that is charged on the loan. This means that the customer will repay back the exact amount of money that they borrowed if they are successfully granted a loan.

However, a commission fee starting from 12% is charged for each next loan. It is important to note that the customers will cover the mobile money carrier fees when paying the loan.
