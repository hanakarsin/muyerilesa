Employee Earns over KSh 70m in 15 Years from Job He Quit Without Employer's Knowledge

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, March 3, 2024

- The Italian employee had been employed at Ciaccio hospital before he started absenting himself

- The accused was allegedly paid over KSh70 million for the work he never done

- The suspect threatened one of his managers who wanted to file a disciplinary report against him when he realised the x-employee had not been working

Italian hospital employee has been accused of skipping work for 15 years while on full pay without the knowledge of his employer.

According to BBC news, the employee had been working in a local hospital in the southern city of Catanzaro, identified as Ciaccio hospital, before he stopped showing up for work in 2005.

The suspect had been receiving salary for all the 15 years, which summed up to over KSh 70 million, yet he had been away from work illegally.

A lengthy police investigation into the absenteeism and suspected fraud in the public Italian sector led to the arrests of the accused.

Authorities are currently investigating the suspect for fraud, extortion and abuse of office.

He was also accused of threatening his manager, who wanted to file a disciplinary report against him when they realised that he had not been working.

Six managers at the hospital were also being investigated in connection with the alleged absenteeism of that one employee.
